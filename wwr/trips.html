Family Fun Float

Perfect for beginners and kids. A scenic half-day float with gentle Class I–II rapids, riverside snacks, and plenty of photo stops.

  • Duration: Half Day (4 hrs)
  • Difficulty: Easy (I–II)
  • Season: May–September
  • Price: $90 / person

Canyon Run

Our most popular full-day adventure. Enjoy steady Class II–III rapids, a riverside picnic lunch, and sweeping canyon vistas.

  • Duration: 1 Day (7–8 hrs)
  • Difficulty: Moderate (II–III)
  • Season: April–October
  • Price: $120 / person

Rapids Rush

Two days of adrenaline on pushy Class III–IV water. Includes pro guides, camp gear, meals, and unforgettable starry nights.

  • Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night
  • Difficulty: Hard (III–IV)
  • Season: June–August
  • Price: $250 / person
Book Rapids Rush

Sunset Float

A tranquil evening float ideal for couples and photographers. Calm water, golden light, and a toast at take-out.

  • Duration: 2.5 hrs (evening)
  • Difficulty: Easy (I–II)
  • Season: May–September
  • Price: $80 / person
